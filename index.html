What does PID stand for? What is the purpose of the P, the I, and the D? What steps did you take to choose the best values for your robot? Explain this like you would for someone taking this course next year.

  PID stands for proportional integral derivative. P-control linearly correlates the controller output to the error, I-control correlates the controller output to the integral of the error, and D-control correlates the controller output to the derivative of the error. We decided that I and D were not necessarily needed to create a functioning robot so we decided to solely focus on P and we got our value through testing.
